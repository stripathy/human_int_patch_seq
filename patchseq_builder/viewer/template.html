Patch-seq Human GABAergic Cell Viewer

Interactive explorer for human cortical GABAergic interneurons profiled with Patch-seq (simultaneous electrophysiology, transcriptomics, and morphology). Patch-seq cells were collected from resected neocortical human neurosurgical tissues. The snRNA-seq reference was collected from post-mortem donors.
